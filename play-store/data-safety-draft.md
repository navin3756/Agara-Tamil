# Google Play Data Safety Draft

Use this as the Play Console Data Safety starting point. Confirm the final answers inside Play Console before submission.

## Android App Data Collection

The current Android app runs in local mode and does not require account sign-in.

- Personal information: Not collected by the Android app.
- Financial information: Not collected.
- Location: Not collected.
- Contacts: Not collected.
- Photos/videos/files: Not collected by the app.
- Audio: Microphone access is used only for optional local recitation recording practice. Audio stays on the device as a temporary local browser object URL and is not uploaded by the app.
- App activity: Lesson progress can be stored locally on the device.
- Device identifiers: Not intentionally collected by the app.

## Data Sharing

No Android app user data is intentionally shared with third parties in the current release.

## Security Practices

- Data is not sold.
- The app has no ads and no in-app purchases in the current release.
- Optional web authentication is separate from Android local mode and is covered by the public privacy policy.

## Permissions To Declare

- Internet: Required for the web-based app shell and external learning resources.
- Microphone: Used for optional local recitation practice.

## Children And Families Notes

Agara is an educational app for children and families. Because the app includes KG learning content, complete the Play Console target audience and Families policy questions carefully. For a child-directed release, keep ads disabled, keep sign-in optional or unavailable on Android, and ensure all linked content is appropriate.
