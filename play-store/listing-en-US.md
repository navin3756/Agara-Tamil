# Agara Tamil Learning

## App Details

App name: Agara Tamil Learning

Short description: Playful Tamil lessons, KG handbook practice, grammar, speaking, and vocab.

Category: Education

Tags to consider: Education, Language Learning, Kids, Tamil

## Full Description

Agara Tamil Learning helps children build Tamil confidence through gentle, structured weekly practice.

Start with the Kindergarten handbook path for letters, sounds, classwork, home practice, speaking prompts, and projects. Older learners can practice grammar, vocabulary, sentence building, reading, dictation, Thirukkural recitation, phonetics, and project planning.

Why families may love Agara:

- Kindergarten content organized week by week from the Tamil handbook.
- Tamil letters and practice words shown in large, child-friendly cards.
- Grammar and sentence activities that work without a paid AI API.
- Local practice mode on Android, so children can keep learning without sign-in.
- Optional web sign-in support for progress features where available.
- No ads and no in-app purchases in the current release.

Agara is designed as a parent-guided Tamil learning companion for families and classrooms.
